# Customer Behavior & RFM Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model to help the business identify valuable customers, improve retention, and increase revenue.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow using SQL, Python, and Excel.

---

## Business Problem

The company has thousands of customers but treats them all the same.

Without customer segmentation:

- Marketing budget is wasted.
- Loyal customers receive no special attention.
- Customers leave without being noticed.
- Revenue opportunities are missed.

---

## Business Questions

1. Which customers generate the highest revenue?
2. Which customers are likely to stop purchasing?
3. How should marketing campaigns differ between customer segments?

---

## Dataset

Online Retail Dataset

- Transactions: 541,909
- Customers: 4,372
- Countries: 38
- Period: Dec 2010 – Dec 2011
